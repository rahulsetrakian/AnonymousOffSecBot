Cybersecurity is a highly sought-after field in IT, and many people are interested in learning about it. But sometimes, knowing where to start or how to approach ethical hacking can be challenging. I'll help you start from scratch and take you to an advanced level, and please remember to follow every step without skipping.

		b. Linux Fundamentals - https://www.coursera.org/learn/linux-fundamentals
		c. Linux Essentials for Ethical Hackers ~  freeCodeCamp.org - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1hvVcEhcbLM
	2. Bash(Programming Language):
		a. Hands-on Introduction to Linux Commands and Shell Scripting - https://www.coursera.org/learn/hands-on-introduction-to-linux-commands-and-shell-scripting
		b. Bash Scripting Full Course 3 Hours ~  linuxhint - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e7BufAVwDiM
	2. Python(Programming Language):
		a. Python for Everybody - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8DvywoWv6fI&t=35s
		b. Python Tutorial for Beginners - https://www.simplilearn.com/tutorials/python-tutorial
